Importance

Web development is the process of creating websites that allow users to view and interact with content over the Internet.

Web development includes many different aspects

including the creation of a website design

and code to create a functioning website

Web development is a critical part of bringing business ideas to life. In the 21st century, a solid web presence is essential for any business. This is because it gives potential customers a glimpse into the company culture and values, whilst also helping with branding and marketing. It also provides customers with a consistent experience across different platforms. Web development is also important because it can help you stand out from competitors who are not as tech-savvy. If your website looks dated or unprofessional, customers may be turned off by your brand, even if they are interested in your products or services.

Web development can also help customers build relationships with your business. For example, social media platforms can
        be used to share news about new products or services. This helps build trust between you and your customers because
        they will feel like they are getting inside information.
